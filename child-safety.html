VibZura Child Safety Standards

VibZura is committed to protecting minors and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Age Requirement

VibZura is intended only for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly allow minors to use the platform.

Prevention Measures

  • In-app reporting of inappropriate behavior
  • Immediate action on reported violations
  • Account suspension or removal for violations

Reporting & Enforcement

Any content involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM) is strictly prohibited. VibZura complies with all applicable local and international child safety laws and reports to appropriate authorities when required.

User Reporting

Users can report safety concerns directly within the VibZura app.
